My Agendas

⏰
Lead by example through punctuality, responsibility, and respect
I will consistently follow school rules, be on time, and maintain respectful behavior to inspire others by actions—not just words.
🤝
Be an approachable and reliable representative for students
I will always be available for my classmates to talk to, whether it’s about a concern, an idea, or just someone to listen.
📣
Communicate student concerns informally to teachers or staff
Even if there’s no formal system, I’ll do my best to pass on genuine concerns or suggestions to teachers respectfully when possible.
🎉
Celebrate and appreciate classmates through kind words and recognition
I’ll make sure small efforts and acts of kindness by students don’t go unnoticed—whether it’s a compliment or a quick shout-out in class.
📬
Spread school updates and important info through face-to-face interactions
I’ll ensure classmates are aware of important things happening in school by sharing updates directly, especially when no official announcements are made.
📰
Assist teachers and staff during school events and assemblies
I’ll actively help during school functions, assemblies, and programs, keeping things organized and supporting both teachers and students.
🎭
Encourage respectful behavior and unity among students
I’ll promote teamwork and kindness among classmates, reduce misunderstandings, and help make school a friendly place for everyone.
🌟
Help create a positive, welcoming atmosphere in school
I’ll maintain a cheerful and encouraging attitude, support students who feel left out, and help reduce negativity wherever I can.
🤗
Be consistently present, visible, and supportive as a leader
I’ll be active around the school—during breaks, events, and transitions—so anyone who needs help can easily reach out to me.
💬
Treat every student equally, ensuring everyone feels included and respected
Whether someone is my best friend or not, I’ll treat everyone fairly and respectfully—because a true leader stands for all.

Questions & Answers

What will you do to ensure every student's voice is heard?
I will set up a suggestion box and hold regular meetings to discuss student concerns and ideas.
How will you promote school spirit?
I plan to organize fun events and activities that encourage participation and celebrate our school community.
What is your approach to communication with staff?
I will ensure transparent communication by sharing updates and feedback from students with the staff regularly.
